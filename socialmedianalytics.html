Social Media Analytics

Advantage of social media over traditional surveys is the immediate accessibility of data. Analyzing opioid-related social media posts has the potential to reveal patterns of opioid abuse at a national scale, and understand opinions of the public and opioid users. We have performed various analysis on social media, including spatial-temporal trends of opioid use, content analysis, types of users and their background, and suicide intention.
